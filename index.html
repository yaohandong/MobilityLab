About Mobility Lab

Mobility Lab aims at creating safe and smart transportation systems. Our research focuses on leveraging advanced technologies, including Connected and Autonomous Vehicles, Artificial Intelligence, and Digital Twins, to enhance transportation safety, efficiency, and sustainability. Through interdisciplinary collaboration and data-driven approaches, we strive to develop innovative solutions for real-world mobility challenges.

We collaborate with local governments, organizations, industry, and other universities on several projects and proposals. Our partners include:
